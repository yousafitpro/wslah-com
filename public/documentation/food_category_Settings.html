Food & Category Settings

How to change categories sequence.

Click on Categories showing like this.

  1. Change categories sequence:

    Drag category >> Drop to the sequence position category.

How to change foods sequence.

Click on Foods showing like this.

  1. Change foods sequence:

    Select >> Category >> Drag foods >> Drop to the sequence position food.

    You can not drag & drop foods without select category.
